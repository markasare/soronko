Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What types of disability care services do you provide?
    Answer: We offer a wide range of disability care services, including personal care, in-home support, respite care, transportation assistance, and more. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual.
  2. How can I request disability care services for myself or a loved one?
    Answer: Requesting our services is easy. Simply contact our team through our website or give us a call. We will guide you through the process, assess your needs, and create a personalized care plan.
  3. Are your caregivers trained and qualified?
    Answer: Yes, our caregivers are highly trained, certified, and experienced in providing disability care. They undergo regular training to stay updated on best practices and ensure the safety and well-being of our clients.

  5. Can I choose my caregiver?
    Answer: Yes, we understand the importance of compatibility. We work with you to select a caregiver whose skills and personality align with your preferences and needs.
  6. What is the cost of disability care services?
    Answer: The cost varies based on the type and duration of care needed. We offer transparent pricing and can discuss payment options, including government funding and insurance, to make our services affordable.
  7. Is emergency care available?
    Answer: Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care services. Our dedicated team is ready to assist you in case of any urgent situation. You can reach our emergency hotline at 0414 022 529.
  8. How do I schedule care services?
    Answer: Scheduling is flexible. You can arrange care services on a regular basis or as needed. Contact us, and we will work with you to create a care schedule that suits your lifestyle.
  9. Do you offer support for family caregivers?
    Answer: Absolutely! We understand that family caregivers need support too. We provide respite care services to give family caregivers a break while ensuring your loved one receives the care they need.
  10. What areas do you serve?
    Answer: We proudly serve Australia. Our dedicated team is committed to providing high-quality disability care to our community.
  11. How can I get started with your disability care services?
    Answer: Getting started is simple. Contact us today to discuss your needs, schedule a consultation, and take the first step toward receiving compassionate and professional disability care.
