Our services

Activities of Daily Living (ADL)

Our support team is dedicated to assisting you with a broad range of activities of daily living including eating, bathing, cooking, household cleaning, medication administration, and shopping. We deeply appreciate and respect that everyone's needs are unique. Therefore, our primary objective is to provide personalised assistance in daily personal activities, fostering independent living both within the comfort of your home and in the wider community.

Accommodation & SIL

Our Supported Independent Living (SIL) services are tailored to empower you to lead life on your own terms. Whether you live independently or in shared housing, our comprehensive services cover a wide range of daily living activities. These include assistance with cooking, cleaning, and laundry, support with medication management and medical appointments, and facilitation of access to social and recreational activities. We are also here to assist you if you are seeking accommodation that suits your specific needs and requirements. Currently, we have a new 4-bedroom house available for rent in Taylor, Australian Capital Territory.

Community nursing services

Our specialised staff and qualified community nurses are well trained and highly experienced in addressing the needs of individuals with complex health conditions, including but not limited to:

  • ✔ Complex Bowel Care
  • ✔ PEG Feeding and Management
  • ✔ Support after a hospital stay and post-operative care
  • ✔ Monitoring vitals such as blood pressure, pulse, temperature, and blood sugar levels
  • ✔ Urinary Catheter Management
  • ✔ Subcutaneous Injections
  • ✔ Health assessments and assistance with developing treatment plans
  • ✔ Stoma care
  • ✔ Medication Management
  • ✔ Diabetes management
  • ✔ Assistance with self-care activities

Assistance with travel and transportation

We acknowledge that connecting people with services is a fundamental aspect of leading a fulfilling life. Our reliable transportation services ensure safe and hassle-free commuting, fostering connections within the broader community.

Social and community participation

Our support services extend to helping you participate in structured group-based recreational activities. We also facilitate individualised pursuits such as going to the movies, visiting places you enjoy, engaging in sports, acquiring new skills, and reconnecting with friends and family.
